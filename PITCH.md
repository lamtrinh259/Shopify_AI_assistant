# Store Pulse — Pitch Script (2 min)

## Opening Hook (10s)
> "Every Shopify merchant spends 3 hours a day checking dashboards, cross-referencing spreadsheets, and reacting to problems after they've already cost them money. What if your store had an AI CEO that does all of that — in real time — and takes action before you even wake up?"

## The Problem — Commerce Pain (20s)
> "Shopify gives you data, but not decisions. You can see your orders, but you don't know which product will stock out tomorrow. You can see your customers, but you don't know which ones are about to churn. You can see a sales spike, but you don't know if it's a viral moment or bot fraud."
>
> "Merchants are drowning in dashboards but starving for insight."

## The Solution — Store Pulse (15s)
**[Show /pulse — Health Score at 6.9]**

> "Store Pulse is an AI-powered command center for Shopify. One number — the Health Score — tells you exactly how your store is doing right now. Not yesterday. Right now."
>
> "It monitors five dimensions: sales velocity, inventory health, revenue trends, anomaly detection, and customer retention."

## AI in Action — Not Bolted On (30s)

**[Show Smart Alerts]**
> "The AI doesn't just display data — it thinks. It detected that Stainless Water Bottle hasn't sold in 14 days — dead stock. It predicted Minimalist Watch Silver will stock out in 48 hours. It flagged a 4x order spike that could be viral traffic or bot activity."

**[Click Create Discount — show success]**
> "And it acts. One click to create a targeted discount on Shopify. One click to send a win-back email. These aren't mock buttons — they write to the Shopify Admin API in real time."

**[Show AI Chat — ask "How is my store doing?"]**
> "You can ask it anything. It analyzes YOUR data and gives specific recommendations — not generic advice. It's Claude under the hood, with full store context."

## Deep Intelligence Pages (20s)

**[Quick flip through Predictions → Segments → Anomalies]**
> "Stockout predictions with reorder actions. Customer segmentation with one-click win-back campaigns. Real-time anomaly detection with investigation tools. Every page answers one question and gives you a button to act on it."

## Business Potential (15s)
> "There are 4.6 million Shopify stores. The average merchant pays $29-299/month for Shopify but spends hours manually doing what Store Pulse automates. This is a $50-200/month SaaS product — installed as a Shopify app, connected in 30 seconds, value delivered in the first minute."
>
> "Our moat is the AI layer. The health score algorithm, the predictive engine, the autonomous actions — these compound over time as the AI learns each store's patterns."

## The Close (10s)
> "Store Pulse turns every Shopify merchant into a data-driven operator. No analytics degree required. No spreadsheets. Just one AI CEO that never sleeps."
>
> **"Your store never sleeps. Neither does Store Pulse."**
